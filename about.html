About Me

I’m a junior at Carleton College studying Computer Science and Neuroscience. I have experience in computer science research, software development, and design, and I'm excited to learn more!

I recently interned as a front-end engineer at AlphaScenario, where I designed and developed features for their personalized learning platform. I enjoyed finding the most effective ways for students and teachers to use the platform to facilitate learning.

Last summer, I did research with Dr. Csizmar Dalal on her project Trust and Expertise in Technical Support Relationships, analyzing a dataset of over 90,000 troubleshooting tickets submitted to Carleton’s ITS HelpDesk. My research partner and I implemented an algorithm to identify duplicate questions within the dataset. The algorithm can uncover insights about trust and expertise and improve support services.

I also work as Course Staff and Lab Assistant in the CS Department at Carleton. I enjoy helping students with their coursework in the lab and collaborating with professors to provide feedback on students' assignments.

On campus, I’m a member of the Women’s Soccer Team, Carleton’s Hack4Impact Chapter, and a program lead for TOPSoccer.

 

I am currently looking for a research position in human-computer interaction or an internship in front-end development for Summer 2025!
